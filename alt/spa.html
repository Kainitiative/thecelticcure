Benefits of Japanese Head Spa

  • By toning the scalp and stimulating lymph drainage, it gives you the appearance of a 'face lift' and a better complexion

The Power of Regular Facials

You already know how wonderful a facial feels if you've ever had one. You feel refreshed and at ease after having your skin cleaned, exfoliated, and massaged. Regular facials, however, might genuinely enhance the long-term health of your skin rather than just providing a momentary boost of wellbeing.

A monthly facial is great, unless you are seeking to treat a specific skin condition (acne, hyper-pigmentation, etc.), in which case you would need to schedule several treatments. The rationale behind monthly facials is that your stratum corneum, or outermost layer of skin that is visible, will regenerate entirely around every 28 days.

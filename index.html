Hair Treatment / PRP

Concerned about hair loss or looking for skin rejuvenation? Protein-rich plasma (PRP) treatments are a non-invasive way to provide stimulation to the body to enable re-growth of hair, and to tighten facial skin using protein-rich plasma extracted from the patient's own blood. This plasma contains growth factors that stimulate the production of collagen.

Benefits of Japanese Head Spa

  • By toning the scalp and stimulating lymph drainage, it gives you the appearance of a 'face lift' and a better complexion
  • Hair loss is prevented, and healthy hair growth is encouraged
  • Stimulates the pressure points directly related to various regions of your body, resulting in a feeling of overall well-being
  • Increases 'feel-good hormones' in the body, such as serotonin, dopamine, and endorphin, while lowering cortisol
  • Rebuilds the hair cuticle from the inside out to repair damaged hair
  • Migraines and tight neck/shoulders caused by cranial/physical strain are relieved

The Power of Regular Facials

You already know how wonderful a facial feels if you've ever had one. You feel refreshed and at ease after having your skin cleaned, exfoliated, and massaged. Regular facials, however, might genuinely enhance the long-term health of your skin rather than just providing a momentary boost of wellbeing.

A monthly facial is great, unless you are seeking to treat a specific skin condition (acne, hyper-pigmentation, etc.), in which case you would need to schedule several treatments. The rationale behind monthly facials is that your stratum corneum, or outermost layer of skin that is visible, will regenerate entirely around every 28 days.

Your skin cells are continuously being reproduced during this period from the treated cells in order to replenish the lost cells. Giving your skin a head start, such as a wonderful facial, can encourage your skin cells to reproduce the healthier cells, improving the look of your complexion.
